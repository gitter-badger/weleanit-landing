Concept

We are developpers, community or marketing managers, students. We are all entrepreneurs. We care products, we love projects, we are found of technology.
So, what about having a funny week-end to create new products? Meeting new people? Engaging new challenges? Discovering new technologies?

Following the lean startup methodology, we imagine new products. We lean them by keeping the main features to test they work, and create them in 2 days. Short? No. Enough to focus on the essentials.

Those products have nothing to do with our daily work: we want to experiment. Everyday-life projects, funny ones, sometimes stupid. Whatever they are, we do.

Once done, the products are shared with everybody: most of the time, we provide a demo website, and we always release the source code under an open source licence.
